About Our Best Dentists in Meerut

Our advanced dental clinic in Meerut is built on clinical excellence, ethics, and a patient-first approach led by some of the best dentists in Meerut. We provide comprehensive treatment plans for dental implants, root canal treatment, braces, cosmetic dentistry, and more-tailored to each smile. Serving Shastri Nagar, Jagriti Vihar, Ganga Nagar, Pallavpuram, Modipuram, Kanker Khera, Meerut Cantt, and all nearby areas.

Meet Our Best Dentists & Specialists

Founder & Lead Consultant

Dr. Shilpi Jain

Dr. Shilpi Jain is a dedicated and compassionate dental professional, renowned for her meticulous approach to patient care and commitment to clinical excellence. As the Founder of Shree Rishabh Dev Dental Care and a BDS graduate from Subharti Dental College, she combines advanced dental knowledge with a gentle, patient-first philosophy. Dr. Jain is known for her expertise in aesthetic and family dentistry, ensuring every patient receives personalized treatment in a welcoming environment.

Co-founder & Chief Implant Surgeon

Dr. Sachiin Gupta

Dr. Sachiin Gupta is a specialist in dental implants, bone grafting surgeries, and soft tissue cosmetic procedures, with over 16 years of extensive clinical experience. He has served at LLRM Medical Hospital and as Assistant Professor at Venkateshwara Medical College. Currently, he is associated with Subharti Dental College & Hospital and Moolchand Dental Hospital, and is the Chief Implant Surgeon at Shree Rishabh Dev Dental Care and Implant Centre. Dr. Gupta also consults at various clinics across the NCR region, bringing advanced implantology and surgical expertise to every case.

Specialist Orthodontist

Dr. Ankur Agarwal

Dr. Ankur Agarwal is an accomplished Orthodontist with over 10 years of experience in India and Dubai. He is a certified Invisalign provider, a fellow of the Pierre Fauchard Academy (USA), and one of the few certified Sports Dentists in India. Dr. Agarwal has a special interest in cleft orthodontics, dentofacial orthopaedics, and sleep apnea management, and has contributed to several scientific publications and dental programs internationally.

Gold Medalist, Endodontist

Dr. Anuja Kaushik

Dr. Anuja Kaushik is a Gold Medalist and specialist in Conservative Dentistry and Endodontics, recognized for her expertise in advanced root canal treatments, restorative dentistry, and dental aesthetics. With a strong focus on preserving natural teeth and restoring smiles, she utilizes modern microscopic techniques and advanced technology. Dr. Kaushik is dedicated to delivering precise, patient-centered care, ensuring comfort and excellence in every treatment.
