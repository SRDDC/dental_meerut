Full Mouth Implant Rehabilitation — Case Study

Comprehensive full mouth implant treatment performed at our Meerut clinic. The patient underwent complete rehabilitation with implant-supported restorations to restore function and aesthetics.

Treatment Summary

The treatment included careful case assessment, 3D imaging for implant planning, staged implant placement with augmentation where required, and final implant-supported crowns to restore occlusion and aesthetics. The patient achieved improved chewing ability, speech, and confidence.
