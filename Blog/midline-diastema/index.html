Midline Diastema Closure with Porcelain Veneers

A conservative cosmetic approach to close the midline gap while preserving natural tooth structure.

Treatment Summary

We performed a comprehensive smile analysis and shade selection. A minimally invasive veneer preparation conserved enamel while allowing ideal contouring to close the diastema and maintain tooth proportions. The final restorations were polished for a natural texture and shade match.

Clinical Notes

  • Shade matched to adjacent teeth
  • Conservative enamel preparation
  • High-strength porcelain veneers for longevity
  • Post-op instructions and monitoring
