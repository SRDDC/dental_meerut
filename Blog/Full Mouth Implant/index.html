Full Mouth Implant Rehabilitation — Case Study

Comprehensive implant rehabilitation to restore function and aesthetics.

Treatment Summary

Case planning included 3D imaging, evaluation of bone volume, and staged surgical placement of implants with appropriate grafting. Restorations were fabricated to restore occlusion and esthetics, providing the patient with improved masticatory function and facial support.

Clinical Notes

  • CBCT-guided implant planning
  • Site augmentation as needed
  • Custom abutments and definitive crowns
  • Maintenance and follow-up schedule
