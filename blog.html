Case Blog: Midline Diastema Closure with Veneers

This case involved a visible midline spacing (midline diastema) between the upper front teeth. The patient wanted a natural, symmetrical smile without bulky restoration.

After smile analysis and shade planning, we prepared a conservative veneer treatment plan. Minimal enamel reduction was done, impressions were recorded, and custom veneers were designed to close the gap while preserving natural tooth proportions.

At final delivery, the veneers were trialed for fit and aesthetics, then bonded using adhesive protocol for long-term stability. The result is a balanced smile line, closed diastema, and improved confidence with a natural look.
